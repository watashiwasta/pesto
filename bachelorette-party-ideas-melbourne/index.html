11 Bachelorette Party Ideas For An Event To Remember 

Hold onto your veils because Melbourne is about to become the backdrop for the bachelorette bash of a lifetime!

Monro Grier - Staff Writer • October 17, 2024

SHARE THE ARTICLE

Planning a memorable bachelorette party can be a difficult and time consuming task. If you’re involved in the wedding preparations too, you might be feeling the pressure. Luckily we have rounded up some of the coolest places that offer activities that are slightly out of the ordinary. Get ready to paint the town red, at the ultimate bachelorette and prepare to make more memories than your phone can handle. Here are a few bachelorette party ideas for an event to remember. 

1. Alcotraz

We normally can’t endorse anything that’ll put you behind bars, but we’ll make an exception for Alcotraz. An immersive, Prohibition-themed bar, this place will mix up delightful drinks and add an element of free-spirited fun, as you’ll need to smuggle liquor past the guards and evade the watchful eyes of the warden to sip your drinks in peace. The bar offers the option of special occasion bookings, and even private hire if your bachelorette party is big enough. Check it out here.

2. Magic Men Live

Get ready for an unparalleled night out featuring Australia’s top-ranked male revue show, Magic Men, in their production “Feel The Magic”. Witness the most scorching performances as these stunning hunks bring the heat. Enjoy a glass of sparkling wine, savour complimentary finger food served by attractive waiters, and get ready to witness the Magic Men in action. This extraordinary show guarantees to leave you breathless with its heart-stopping live performance.

3. IceBar Melbourne

Escape to a winter wonderland at the coolest bar in Melbourne! Get kitted out in provided snow gear, and stay cozy in the ambient temperatures of -10° at Australia’s only ice bar. With premium cocktails such as Tipsy Eskimo and Snowstorm as well as ice shots such as Frostbite and The Chill, you’ll be warming up from the inside in no time. 

4. Bottomless Brunch

What better way to celebrate the start of a new life chapter than over brunch and endless drinks? Luckily for Melbourne, there are more than a few bottomless brunches around town and to help you choose one for your next gathering of friends, here are six of our favourite bottomless brunch spots.

5. Luxury Chauffeured Wine Tour

After getting picked up by your personal chauffeur for the day, you will be transported to De Bortoli Wine Estate. You’ll then be taken on a wine tour by a local guide, who can answer all your wine-related questions. Then look forward to a 2-course meal (we can’t let you drink on an empty stomach) as well as two premium wine-tasting events. You’ll leave the vineyard feeling like a real connoisseur!

6. Parisian Bottomless High Tea Experience

Paris is known as one of the most romantic cities in the world, so why not dabble in a bit of Parisian magic before the big day? This high tea experience offers 2 hours of delicious food and drinks, in the most luxurious Parisian-style apartment. You’ll be saying oh la la to the incredible spread of high tea delicacies and French Champagne. Cheers to the big day, and bon appetit! 

7. Mornington Peninsula Food & Wine Taste Trail

Enjoy a full-day itinerary on this 9-hour food and wine tour. This tour package takes care of all the details for you so that all you need to focus on is having a good time with your friends. You can look forward to three top-notch wineries and a craft brewery, complete with a satisfying lunch and scenic vineyard views. Delight in beer gardens, a stunning sculpture park perched on a clifftop, and breathtaking coastal vistas on this incredible day out.

8. Drag Brunch

Add a little glitz and glamour to your casual weekend brunch with an extravagant drag brunch in Melbourne. Expect bottomless drinks, delicious brunch items and fun entertainment from resident drag queens. Round up your bride tribe and get ready for good vibes at one of these wonderful venues serving up drag brunch in Melbourne.

9. Private Karaoke

There’s no time to be a blushing bride at this bachelorette party, because everyone will get their moment in the spotlight! Enjoy a private karaoke room with over 350,000 songs to choose from. Order drinks and cocktails without skipping a beat, and belt out your favourite tunes. With room for up to 25 people, this is sure to be a bachelorette to remember. 

10. Saboteurs

If you loved playing the online multiplayer game, Among Us, you’ll want to check out Saboteurs, an immersive, space-themed game played inside 11 converted shipping containers. As a crew member, you’ll complete tasks around a designated moon base with plenty of features like a Decontamination Unit, Sleep Pods, a Medbay, Mining Tools and more. But, beware! There may be an imposter on the ship. Stop and eject these sinister players before they ‘eliminate’ you and your crew.

11. SENSAS

SENSAS is a one-of-a-kind, immersive sensory experience where you and your team are taken on a wild journey through challenges designed to test and engage your five senses in ways you’ve never imagined. We’re talking about two hours of pure, exhilarating fun in the “Sensory Labs” that will leave you and your group buzzing with excitement. 

For a more sedate celebration, check out these fun themed cafes in Melbourne!
